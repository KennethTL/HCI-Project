DESTINATIONS

KENYA

Kenya is a country in East Africa with coastline on the Indian Ocean. Kenya is the destination for you if you seek an adventure that is soaked in an ancient colorful culture and peppered with rare natural sights.

Famed as the world center for animal safaris and active adventure holidays, Kenya is an absolute must-visit for the wildlife enthusiast.

Learn More

UGANDA

Uganda is a multicultural country in East Africa dubbed 'The Pearl of Africa' by Churchil Winston.Its diverse landscape encompasses the snowcapped Rwenzori Mountains and immense Lake Victoria - the principal source of the great River Nile.

It is abundant in wildlife such as Chimpanzees, Gorillas, famous Big 5 at the 10 Savannah parks, and 1000+ rare bird species.

Learn More

TANZANIA

Tanzania is an East African country known for its vast wilderness areas.

They include the plains of Serengeti National Park, a safari mecca populated by the “big five” game (elephant, lion, leopard, buffalo, rhino), and Kilimanjaro National Park, home to Africa’s highest mountain.

Learn More

SOMALIA

Somalia, The Horn of Africa, is a country located on the furthest east of Africa. It borders the Indian Ocean hence has a vast coastline with many beaches.

This country has a very rich history and very magnificent sites that are hidden and undervisited. It has a vast array of undiscovered beaches and coral reefs and is a great destination for beach lovers.

Learn More

BURUNDI

With great culture, hospitable hosts, and historical tourist sites, Burundi is a land-​locked country just waiting to be experienced.

An important place to visit in Burundi is the southern source of the Nile, which is located in Rutovu district, in southern Burundi. A pyramid has been built there and attracts many visitors.

Learn More

RWANDA

Also known as 'The land of a Thousand Hills', Rwanda is a beautiful french-speaking landlocked country in the Great Rift Valley in East Africa.

It is a country filled with stunning scenery, very warm and welcoming people, filled with extraordinary biodiversity and incredible wildlife. It is reknown worldwide for Gorilla trekking with great views of other animal species.

Learn More

CONGO

The DRC has a number of unique tourist opportunities, including wildlife reserves, indigenous cultures, and geological wonders not found easily or anywhere else in Africa.

12% of the country is made up of protected areas including 7 national parks and 57 reserves areas, with great ecotourism potential.

Learn More

ETHIOPIA

Ethiopia, in the Horn of Africa, is a rugged, landlocked country split by the Great Rift Valley. With archaeological finds dating back more than 3 million years, it’s a place of ancient culture.

It is an ideal destination to all interested in history, be it cultural or religious, as well rich foods and coffee. Dubbed 'The Land of Origins', Ethiopia is also home to one of the earliest forms of man therefore, if history is your interest then Ethiopia is your cup of tea.

Learn More

SOUTH SUDAN

Located in the North-Eastern part of the African continent, the Republic of South Sudan is the country’s newest nation

For nature lovers, South Sudan is the place to head to. The country boasts of four national parks and fourteen game reserves that are home to some of the most spectacular and very important wildlife in Africa.
