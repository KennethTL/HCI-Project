ETHIOPIA

It is an ideal destination to all interested in history, be it cultural or religious, as well rich foods and coffee. Dubbed 'The Land of Origins', Ethiopia is also home to one of the earliest forms of man therefore, if history is your interest then Ethiopia is your cup of tea.

ADDIS ABABA

This city colloquially simply known as Addis is the capital of Ethiopia. It hosts the headquarters of African Union hence is filled with people from all over the world.

Within the city is a world class museum, the Ethiopia National Museum,a hidden gem that displays 'Lucy' an early hominid. Ths city is so historical it boasts of over 7 museums that display in great detail the history and culture of the beautiful country, Ethiopia.

Simien Mountains National Park

This being the largest national park in Ethiopia constitutes the Simien mountains as well as Ras Dashan, the highest point in Ethiopia.It is home to a number of endangered species, including the Ethiopian wolf and the walia ibex, a wild goat found nowhere else in the world.

This destination is known for its unique biosphere with steep cliffs and cool climate as well as wild life that is unique only to Ethiopia.

LALIBELA

Also known as 'Second Jerusalem', Lalibela is easily one of Ethiopia's holiest cities. Lalibela is rich in historical, religious and architectural value.

In Lalibela, there exists 11 churches chiselled out of rock from foundation. THe churches are deep in the ground with their roofs being at ground level. This destination is a centre of pilgrimage to religious tourists, priests and worshippers from all over the world.
