UGANDA

UGANDA

Uganda is a multicultural country in East Africa dubbed 'The Pearl of Africa' by Churchil Winston.Its diverse landscape encompasses the snowcapped Rwenzori Mountains and immense Lake Victoria - the principal source of the great River Nile. It is abundant in wildlife such as Chimpanzees, Gorillas, famous Big 5 at the 10 Savannah parks, and 1000+ rare bird species.

KAMPALA

Kampala is Uganda's capital city located near the banks of Lake Victoria. With some terming it 'The City that never sleeps', Kampala is ever a buzz of activities at all hours hence being bored is not an option.

In Kampala, one can visit Kasubi Tombs,the Parliament of Uganda and the Uganda National Mosque. Its proximity to Lake Victoria allows for rafting, bungee jumping and jet-boating. Kampala is filled with life at night being home to many clubs, eateries and restaurants.

ENTEBBE

Located 35km from the capital is Entebbe, home to Uganda's national airport. Entebbe is home to the Entebbe Botanical Garden and the Entebbe Zoo which is habitat to lots of wildlife.

Also, Entebbe brags East Africa's oldest golf course over 100 years old and playable all year round with additional actvities at the spot.

JINJA

Commonly referred to as 'The Adventure Capital of East Africa', Jinja is located on the shores of Lake Victoria at the source of the White Nile. This fills it with numerous water activities such as white-water rafting, kayaking and bungee jumping.

This little city is famous for sourcing the Great River Nile as well as Mahatma Gandhi monument.
